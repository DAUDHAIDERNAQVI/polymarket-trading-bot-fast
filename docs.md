## How you can get profit (in short)

1. **Connect** your wallet and ensure you have USDC.
2. **Start** the bot and let it find the current BTC 5‑minute market.
3. The bot **places orders** for both UP and DOWN at your chosen price/size.
4. When you have **equal** UP and DOWN positions, the bot can **merge** them back into USDC (you get your cost back minus fees).
5. If one side **wins**, the bot can **redeem** winning tokens for USDC (profit on that side).
6. The bot **repeats** for the next 5‑minute market so you can run the strategy continuously.

For more detail on commands and exact steps inside the bot, use the **Help** section in the bot.
